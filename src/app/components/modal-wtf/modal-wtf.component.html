What is EOS Brands?

EOS Brands is a new EOS game to make money. Know when to invest, play smart and win money.

How it works?
  • The price for creating a new brand is 0.5 EOS
  • The next price is the previous price * 1.40%
  • The 40% inflation ratio splits as follows:
    • 60% goes to the owner as profit
    • 40% splits as follows
      • 40% goes to the total POT
      • 30% goes to dividends between the last 40 buyers
      • 20% goes to the contract
      • 10% goes to the creator of the brand
General rules

The game ends when the game timer reaches 0. The POT will be delivered to last the player who bought a brand.
