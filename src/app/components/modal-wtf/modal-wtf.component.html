What is EOS Brands?

EOS Brands is a new EOS game to make money. Know when to invest, play smart and win money. The rules for each round may change if the community wants. Once a round has started, rules won't change until that round has finished.
If you want to discuss the next round rules please join our telegram group.

Rules for round 3:
  • The price for creating a new brand is 0.1 EOS
  • The next price is the previous price * 1.40%
  • The 40% inflation ratio splits as follows:
    • 60% goes to the owner as profit
    • 40% splits as follows
      • 40% goes to the total POT
      • 20% goes to dividends between the last 40 buyers
      • 20% goes to the contract
      • 20% goes to the creator of the brand

The round starts with a donated POT of 10.0000 EOS and ends when the timer reaches 0. The POT will be delivered to the last player who bought a brand.
